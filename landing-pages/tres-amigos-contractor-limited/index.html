About Us

Tres Amigos Contractor Limited

  • At Tres Amigos Contractor Limited, we're passionate about providing the finest selection of fresh, high-quality fruits and vegetables to households and businesses across New Zealand.
  • With a commitment to excellence and a love for all things fresh and delicious, we've become a trusted name in the industry.
  • We source our fruits and vegetables from trusted local farmers and suppliers, ensuring that every item meets our stringent quality standards.
  • We are committed to sustainable farming and environmentally friendly practices. We aim to reduce our carbon footprint and support growers who share our values.
